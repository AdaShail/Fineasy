FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is FinEasy really free to use?

Yes! Our Free plan is completely free forever. You can create up to 50 invoices per month, manage 25 customers, and access basic analytics. No credit card required.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade 256-bit encryption to protect your data. All information is stored securely in the cloud with automatic backups. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I create GST-compliant invoices?

Yes! FinEasy supports GST invoicing with automatic tax calculations. You can add GSTIN, HSN codes, and generate GST-compliant invoices that meet all regulatory requirements.

Does FinEasy work on mobile?

Yes! FinEasy works on web, Android, and iOS. Your data syncs automatically across all devices, so you can manage your business from anywhere.

Can I send invoices via WhatsApp?

Yes! You can share invoices directly via WhatsApp with one click. You can also send payment reminders to customers through WhatsApp.

How do I upgrade or cancel my plan?

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan anytime from your account settings. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
